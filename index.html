Our Story

A Legacy of Taste in Arlington

Hughes Restaurants LLC was founded with a singular vision: to transform the dining landscape of Arlington by harmonizing local ingredients with world-class culinary techniques. Located on South Meade Street, we have established ourselves as a sanctuary for food enthusiasts who seek more than just a meal, but a memorable experience.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that great food begins with the soil, which is why we partner exclusively with regional farmers and artisans to source the freshest seasonal produce. We meticulously craft every dish to tell a story of flavor, texture, and aroma, ensuring that each guest leaves with a lasting impression of excellence.

From intimate dinners to grand celebrations, our dedicated team of hospitality professionals strives to anticipate every need, providing service that is as refined as our cuisine. We invite you to join us and discover why Hughes Restaurants remains a beacon of gastronomic innovation in the community.

Immerse yourself in an atmosphere of sophistication where our chef's tasting menus take center stage. We offer a rotating seasonal menu paired with an extensive selection of vintage wines. Every detail, from the lighting to the table setting, is curated for your comfort.
